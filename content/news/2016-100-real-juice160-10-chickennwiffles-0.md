Matt Pearson kept things close through two, surrendering only one run in that stretch despite (or perhaps because of) his arcing slowball.  Unfortunately for Matt, his offense did not get going before the Juicers got their juice flowing.  Sam Lacroix belted the first homer of the year, and went 5-6.  Matt Rosenfeld allowed only 3 hits in his shutout performance.
