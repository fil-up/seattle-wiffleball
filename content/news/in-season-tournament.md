The 2nd Annual Seattle Wiffle In-Season Tournament is coming up! Join us for a day of competitive play and camaraderie. This tournament brings together teams from across the league for an exciting day of wiffleball action.

## Tournament Details

- **Date**: TBD
- **Location**: Cowen Park
- **Format**: Double elimination
- **Entry Fee**: $50 per team

## What to Expect

This tournament brings together teams from across the league for an exciting day of wiffleball action. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or a newcomer to the league, there's a place for you in this tournament.

## Registration

To register your team, please contact the league office or visit our registration page. Spots are limited, so don't wait too long to secure your team's place in this exciting event.
