Get A Wiff 19  , West Coast Washout 17  


The defending champs started the season off in a deep hole, going down 12-2 before beginning to rally in what would become the most unexpectedly close game of the day.  Offseason acquisition Brian Pendergast, Austin Cudworth, and Kevin Tsuchida all homered to cut the deficit to 16-14.  Get a Wiff countered with three homers of their own, one by Otto Par and two by former Wiff Waffer Andrew Ybarra.  Par survived the bloodbath to record his first victory on the mound.


 West Coast Washout 4,   Get A Wiff 0  
 


Greg Nyssen kept the wolves at bay, allowing just four runs on 13 hits, but the bats were unable to solve Dan Rish's pitching, as he fanned 18 on the day.  Nyssen put up the only hit of the contest for Get a Wiff, a single to lead of the fourth.  Four Washout players recorded three or more hits: Matt Kane, Andrew Winter, Austin Cudworth, and Dan Rish.
