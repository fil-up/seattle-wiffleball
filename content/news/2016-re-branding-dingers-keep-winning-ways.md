Dingers&#160;  10,&#160;  Wiffle Junction    &#160;0  


After reaching base on a fielder's choice in the third, Doug Connelly capitalized on the chance to play spoiler to Aidan Welch's no-hit bid. &#160;One out away from losing by the mercy rule, Doug poked a single up the middle to stave off the goose egg. &#160;Nick Winn remains the only pitcher in the league to have thrown a no-hitter, with two in 2015. &#160;Welch also recorded nine total bases in five at-bats against Jeremy Martin, formerly of the Kalamazoo Wiffle League.


  Dingers&#160;  14,&#160;  Wiffle Junction    &#160;4    &#160;  


BJ Macasey belted a lead-off home run in the 2nd to give Wiffle Junction a 1-0 in Game 2, but the lead did not hold for long. &#160;Colin Fallon's three-run single highlighted a 7-run third inning to give the Dingers the lead. &#160;The gap was cut to 7-4 on a Jeremy Martin home run in the 4th, which translated two walks into runs. &#160;Despite allowing eleven walks, Jacob Pope scattered them too well to be seriously hurt by them, and he also mixed in eleven strikeouts. &#160;The combination of BJ Lemon and Danny Sizemore pitched well for the Junction, allowing only six walks, but the Dingers pounded out fifteen hits.&#160;
