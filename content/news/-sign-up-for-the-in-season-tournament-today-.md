The 2nd Annual Seattle Wiffle In-Season Tournament is in less than two weeks! From 10am to 6pm on Sunday June 29th, Cowen Park will host two fields for a chaotic scramble to the $100 per player grand prize.

                     
                     
                        
The Tournament is your opportunity to join forces with players from across the league under the leadership of a one-day captain. It’ll be a full schedule of high-octane action and easy camaraderie, from the skills competition for seeding to time-limited short-mound games and home run derby tiebreakers. Just for participating, you will receive free Seattle Wiffle merchandise and have all-day access to the finest Costco-sourced concessions.

                     
                     
                        
All past and present Seattle Wiffle players are automatically eligible for the Tournament — the only requirement is that you fill out THIS FORM by 9pm on Thursday June 26th. (Friends and family are welcome to participate, just have them fill out the form and submit a $30 entry fee.) The League Office will then create the bracket and select captains to pick the teams, which will be revealed the morning of the tournament.
