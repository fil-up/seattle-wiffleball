RBI Steaks 13, Chicken'n'Wiffles 8 


In their league debut, the RBI Steaks had a back-and-forth tussle with Chicken'n'Wiffles, but put the game away with 5 big runs in the fourth. &#160;Two walks and a single by Matt Leonard led off the inning, and then things started falling apart for Matt Pearson and C'n'W. &#160;After a Reilly Brindle sac fly, the next four Steaksmen singled, putting their team on top for good. &#160;Andy Siegel went 5-6 with a double and 3 RBI for the losers. &#160;Chicken'n'Wiffles had battled back from an early 6-2 deficit, but were unable to overcome the power of the RBI's many RBIs.


 RBI Steaks 20, Chicken'n'Wiffles 9 


Chicken'n'Wiffle starter Liam Munro suffered his first defeat of the season. &#160;Though he pitched largely the same way he had against the West Coast Washout in week 1, pitching to contact and limiting walks, the defense behind him wasn't as sharp on Sunday. &#160;Reilly Brindle and Matt Leonard each homered for the victors, and Elliot Price went 4-4 with 2 doubles. &#160;Team captain Jimmy Froio went the distance on the mound for his first win, and legged out a triple in 4th to help his own cause.
