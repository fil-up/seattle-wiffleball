  Pitcher of the Week: Matt Leonard  


 Leonard came on in relief with a one-run lead in the fourth, and slammed the door shut on the Washout, while his own team kept on scoring, and struck out seven batters in the process. &#160;While the Washout threatened in all three innings, Leonard delivered in the clutch, stranding at least two runners in each of those innings to earn the save. 

 
 
 
 &#160;  
 

  Defensive Player of the Week: Luke Lawson  


 The elder Lawson created some sort of vacuum from his third-base position against Wiff Waff, catching pop-up after pop-up in fair and foul territory. &#160;The only miscue Lawson was involved in was when teammate Cody Thomson ignored Lawson's call for the ball, resulting in a pop-up hitting the deck. &#160;Lawson's good defense was key in allowing the Dinger Squad two wins by comfortable margins.
