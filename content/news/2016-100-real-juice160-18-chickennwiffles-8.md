100% Real Juice scored eleven in an excruciatingly long first inning which saw ten walks followed by a two-run double by Adam Brickett. &#160;Epo Olivarez recorded his team's first win, but it was not easy. &#160;The scrappy Chicken'n'Wiffles offense tried to claw its way back into the game, but their efforts were cut short by Gabe Showalter's mercy-clinching walk in the 5th.
