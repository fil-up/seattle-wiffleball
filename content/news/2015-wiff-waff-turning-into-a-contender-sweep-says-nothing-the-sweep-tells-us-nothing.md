RBI Steaks 15, Wiff Waff 1 


For the fourth consecutive week, Wiff Waff found themselves involved in a sweep. &#160;Twice they have been on the losing end. &#160;In the opener, the RBI Steaks quickly dealt a merciful end. &#160;Reilly Brindle struck out nine in the win, and Nick Winn went 4-5 with two doubles and a homerun. &#160;Each RBI Steak reached base at least three times. &#160;Andrew Ybarra made some of the lone highlights for Wiff Waff defensively. &#160;His sliding catch in the 3rd to rob Brindle of a hit was deemed "Torii Hunteresque" by his opponents.


&#160;


 RBI Steaks 10, Wiff Waff 0 


 After being held scoreless for two innings, the RBI Steaks broke it open versus Craig McGrath and Wiff Waff, stringing together hits and quickly turning them into runs. &#160;McGrath had the lone hit against Nick Winn, who buckled down after allowing the first three runners of the game to reach base. &#160;The one run given up in the series by the RBI Steaks is the league-wide low to date.
